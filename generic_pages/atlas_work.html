3. Overview of Workforce:

Atlas Labs has a total of 1,470 employees, comprising 237 past employees and 1,233 current employees.

The attrition rate is 16.1%, suggesting the need for attrition reduction strategies.

4. Hiring Trends:

Historical hiring trends show variations over time, suggesting the need to investigate the causes behind these fluctuations.

5. Demographics Analysis:

Most current employees fall within the 20-29 age group, and a significant portion are married.

6. Attrition Analysis:

Attrition rates vary by hire date, tenure, and travel frequency. Most attrition occurs within the first year of employment.

"Frequent Travelers" have the highest attrition rate, indicating a possible need to investigate this group further.

Summarized findings for quick reference, highlighting the primary trends and challenges.

8. Recommendations:

Suggestions for addressing attrition, improving hiring strategies, and focusing on high-risk areas.

Encourage exit interviews and surveys to gather qualitative data.
