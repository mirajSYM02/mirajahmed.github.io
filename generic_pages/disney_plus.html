Disney+

Movies and Shows Dashboard

The dashboard highlights some details about selective movies as well some general information about the platform.

Details: In this case study, I had to analyze the factors contributing to the high viewership of Disney+ shows and movies.


Action: The following data was analyzed:

  • Shows and movies ratings
  • Description about shows or movies
  • Ratings on different genres
  • Number of views
  • Demographics of viewers

Results: Based on the analysis, the following insights were found:

  • The top shows and movies have ratings of Rated G and PG.
  • The top genres are Animation, Comedy, and Family.
  • Most shows are based in the USA.
  • The platform has more movies than TV shows.

Conclusion: The insights suggest that the high viewership of Disney+ shows and movies is due to the company's strong library of content, its focus on family-friendly programming, and its affordable pricing. Disney+ is well-positioned to continue its success as a popular streaming service.

Recommendations: Here are some additional recommendations for Disney+:

  • Continue to invest in original content.
  • Expand the library of family-friendly programming.
  • Keep pricing affordable.
  • Promote Disney+ as a great way to watch favorite Disney, Pixar, Marvel, Star Wars, and National Geographic movies and shows.

Overall, the Disney+ dashboard is a valuable tool for Disney+ executives. It provides insights into the viewership of their shows and movies and can help them make decisions about future programming.
