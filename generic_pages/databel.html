Databel, Customer Churn Analysis on Tableau

Situation:

In this Tableau case study, I worked with a dataset from a telecom company called Databel. The company had a primary focus on reducing customer churn, which was a top priority for their subscription-based business. My task was to analyze the churn rates and gain insights into why customers were churning at the rate they were. The story that includes four dashboards for several aspects of churning, is fully dynamic dashboards,

Feel free to work with the dashboards..

Task:

Using Tableau, I created several dashboards to analyze the churn rates of Databel, later combined them in a story to show depth of insights. The dashboards included calculated fields and various visualizations, such as dual-axis graphs and scatter plots. My goal was to understand the reasons behind customer churn and identify strategies to reduce it.

The Dashboard details:

  • "The Overview" Page: Designed and implemented a dynamic dashboard featuring two interactive donut pie charts for slicing and filtering data, a versatile bar plot, and an informative map, collectively providing a comprehensive project overview.


  • "Age Brackets and Groups" Page: Constructed an analytical dashboard presenting detailed insights on age groups and demographics through interactive bar plots, enhanced by user-friendly filtering options for a deeper understanding.


  • "Payment and Contract Type" Dashboard: Engineered an insightful dashboard showcasing diverse payment methods and contract types, enriched with interactive filters to facilitate in-depth exploration and analysis.


  • "Data and International Plan" Dashboard: Conceptualized and executed a multi-faceted dashboard utilizing matrix charts and informative cards to effectively communicate insights, complemented by a dynamic filter for seamless data exploration.

Action:

To accomplish this, I utilized Tableau's features and functionalities. I created calculated fields to perform necessary calculations and derived meaningful insights from the data. The visualizations included dynamic elements achieved through the use of filters and parameters. By exploring the data and applying different visualizations, I aimed to uncover patterns and relationships that could explain the churn rates.

Result:

Through my analysis in Tableau, I gained valuable insights into the churn rates of Databel. I identified key factors that contributed to customer churn and discovered potential strategies to reduce it. The visualizations in my dashboard effectively communicated these findings, allowing stakeholders to understand the reasons behind churn and make informed decisions to improve customer retention.
